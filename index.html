YouTube sucks - 28/7/2020

I hate how YouTube does things nowadays. For example, today I was watching a video. YouTube is already known for their trash recommendations, but now I actually got an interesting recommendation. Around it was a blue border that said "Rate this recommendation from 1 to 5" or something like that. I didn't want to rate the recommendation. I tried to click on the video, but it didn't let me. It said "Rate the recommendation and then you get to watch the video." WHAT THE HELL, YOUTUBE? How do I rate a recommendation when I don't know what's in the video other than from the thumbnail and title, which can often be misleading, and then I thought "Fine. I am going to 'rate' this recommendation." Then, guess what? It DID NOT LET ME WATCH THE VIDEO! It completely hid the video. There was no way for me to watch the video. So, lets recap:

  1. I click the video
  2. YouTube wants (forces) me to rate it and then watch it
  3. I rate it
  4. YouTube takes away the video

This makes absolutely no sense. YouTube, Please fix your website :)
